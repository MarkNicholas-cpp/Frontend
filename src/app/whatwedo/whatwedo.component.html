What We Do

The main reason for considering this project was slums, slums have been a major problem in our country. So In this project, we have mainly concentrated on the problems. In this project we mainly focused on two problems

Redevelopment of slums

Monitoring and avoiding the growth of slums in future

  1. We will redevelop the slums by using technology. And the process we follow is user-friendly. Our project mainly works on user opinion only. We will create a user form for the users who are living in the slums.

  2. The people will express their problems to us through our Website. On our Website, we have created multiple options for users. If any one of the users has not satisfied with the options given by us then the user him/herself can type his problem in the description box provided by us.

  3. For visual proof we have given one box for uploading the photos of slums and there is a size limit of photos uploaded by them

  1. For monitoring and avoiding the growth of slums, also we use technology.

  2. We will give access to our agents only. In that form, we have included many problems faced by the slum people.
